GEOMETRICAL CONSTRUCTIONS

Choose any of the following problems

  1. To divide a straight line AB of 140 mm into given number of equal parts say 5.
  2. To inscribe a regular pentagon in a given circle of diameter (say 150mm).
  3. To construct a regular hexagon, with the given side (say 70mm).
  4. To draw in a given equilateral triangle of sides say 100 mm, 3 equal circles, each touching two sides and other two circles.
